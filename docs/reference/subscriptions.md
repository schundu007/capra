## Free Trial

### What's Included

Every new account gets:

- **2 coding problems** — Full solutions with explanations
- **2 system designs** — Architecture diagrams included
- **2 company preps** — Complete preparation documents
- **Unlimited follow-ups** — On the problems you've solved

### Trial Limitations

- No desktop app access
- No Interview Assistant
- Problem limits reset with subscription

### After Trial

When you've used your free allowance:

1. You'll see an **Upgrade** prompt
2. Previous solutions remain accessible
3. Subscribe to continue solving new problems

## Billing & Payment

### Payment Methods

We accept:

- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Debit cards
- Some regional payment methods

### Managing Your Subscription

1. Log in to [capra.cariara.com](https://capra.cariara.com)
2. Navigate to **Settings → Billing**
3. View and manage your subscription

![Billing Settings](../images/billing-settings.png)
*Manage your subscription in Settings*

### Changing Plans

**Upgrade:** Applied immediately, prorated for current period
**Downgrade:** Takes effect at end of current billing period

### Cancellation

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Access continues until period end
4. Data preserved after cancellation

## Credits System

### What Are Credits?

Credits are used for specific premium features:

| Action | Credit Cost |
|--------|-------------|
| Create company prep | 1 credit |
| Regenerate all sections | 1 credit |
| Update single section | 0 credits |
| View existing prep | 0 credits |

### Credit Allocation

| Plan | Monthly Credits |
|------|-----------------|
| Free Trial | First prep free |
| Monthly | 5/month |
| Quarterly Pro | 7/month (21/quarter) |

### Purchasing Additional Credits

1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Credits**
2. Select credit package
3. Complete purchase

| Package | Price | Per Credit |
|---------|-------|------------|
| 5 credits | $15 | $3.00 |
| 10 credits | $25 | $2.50 |
| 25 credits | $50 | $2.00 |

### Credit Expiration

- Subscription credits expire at period end
- Purchased credits never expire
- Credits don't roll over between periods

## Desktop App Access

### Eligibility

Desktop app requires **Quarterly Pro** subscription.

### Installation

1. Log in at [capra.cariara.com](https://capra.cariara.com)
2. Go to **Account → Downloads**
3. Download for your platform
4. Sign in with your account

### Device Management

Your Quarterly Pro license allows **one device** at a time:

1. First sign-in registers the device
2. To switch devices:
   - Go to **Settings → Devices**
   - Click **Deactivate** on current device
   - Sign in on new device

## FAQ

### Can I get a refund?

We offer refunds within 14 days of purchase if you haven't extensively used the service. Contact support@cariara.com.

### What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data is preserved. If you resubscribe, everything will be there.

### Can I pause my subscription?

Not currently. You can cancel and resubscribe when needed.

### Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! Email support@cariara.com with your .edu email for 25% off.

### Can I share my account?

No. Accounts are for individual use only. We detect and prevent account sharing.

### What payment processor do you use?

We use Stripe for secure payment processing. We never see or store your full card number.
