## Overview

Company Prep creates a personalized interview preparation document that includes:

- **Company research** — Culture, values, recent news
- **Behavioral questions** — Company-specific STAR stories
- **Technical focus areas** — What to study based on role
- **Interview format** — What to expect in each round
- **Custom sections** — Your own notes and materials

## Creating a Company Prep

### Step 1: Navigate to Company Prep

1. Click **Preparation** in the mode selector
2. Or use the keyboard shortcut `Cmd+3` / `Ctrl+3`

![Prep Mode](../images/prep-mode-selector.png)
*Selecting Preparation mode*

### Step 2: Enter Company Information

Fill in the details:

| Field | Example | Required |
|-------|---------|----------|
| **Company Name** | Google | Yes |
| **Position** | Senior Software Engineer | Yes |
| **Job Description** | Copy/paste the JD | Recommended |
| **Your Resume** | Upload or paste | Recommended |
| **Interview Date** | March 15, 2024 | Optional |

![Company Input](../images/company-prep-input.png)
*Enter company and role details*

### Step 3: Generate Prep Document

Click **Generate Prep** to create your personalized document.

> **Processing Time:** Generation typically takes 30-60 seconds depending on the amount of information provided.

## Prep Document Sections

### Company Overview

- **About** — What the company does
- **Culture** — Work environment, values
- **Recent News** — Latest developments

- **Key Requirements** — Must-have skills
- **Nice-to-Haves** — Bonus qualifications

Curated questions based on company values:

**Example (Google):**
```
1. Tell me about a time you had to make a decision with incomplete data.
   - Google values: Data-driven decision making
   - Your story: [Auto-generated from resume]

2. Describe a project where you had to collaborate across teams.
   - Google values: Cross-functional collaboration
```

## Behavioral Questions

### The STAR Method

Every behavioral answer should follow:

| Component | Description | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| **S**ituation | Context and background | "At my previous company..." |
| **T**ask | Your responsibility | "I was tasked with..." |
| **A**ction | What you did | "I decided to..." |
| **R**esult | The outcome | "This resulted in..." |

### Company-Specific Questions

Ascend generates questions based on company values:

**Amazon (Leadership Principles):**
- Customer Obsession
- Ownership
- Invent and Simplify
- Learn and Be Curious

**Google:**
- Googleyness
- General Cognitive Ability
- Role-Related Knowledge
- Leadership

**Meta:**
- Move Fast
- Be Bold
- Focus on Long-Term Impact
- Build Social Value

### Practice Mode

Use follow-up to practice your answers:

```
You: "Help me practice the 'Tell me about a challenging project' question"

Ascend: "Let's practice! First, tell me about a challenging project
you worked on. I'll give you feedback on your STAR structure and
suggest improvements."
```

## Using Your Prep

### Before the Interview

1. **Review daily** — Read through your prep document
2. **Practice stories** — Run through behavioral answers
3. **Technical prep** — Work through recommended problems
4. **Mock interviews** — Use Interview Assistant for practice

### During the Interview

- Reference your **Questions to Ask** section
- Remember your prepared **STAR stories**
- Stay calm — you've prepared for this

### After the Interview

1. Update your prep with new information
2. Note questions you weren't prepared for
3. Add follow-up items

## Tips for Best Results

### Provide Complete Information

The more context you give, the better your prep:

- **Full job description** — Copy the entire JD
- **Updated resume** — Include recent experience
- **Specific role details** — Team, level, focus area

### Use the Job Description

The JD is gold for interview prep:

1. **Keywords** — These will come up in interviews
2. **Requirements** — Focus your technical prep here
3. **Responsibilities** — Prepare relevant stories
4. **Nice-to-haves** — Good topics to highlight

### Keep Preps Updated

Before each interview round:

1. Review and update your prep
2. Add new information learned
3. Refine your STAR stories
4. Update questions to ask
